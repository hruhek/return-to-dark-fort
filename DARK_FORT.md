# DARK FORT

*A slightly adapted and translated version of the solo/micro game that became MÖRK BORG.*
Grab a handful of dice, a pencil and paper and begin your perilous delve into the **DARK FORT**.

**Artwork:** David Hoskins
**Layout:** Johan Nohr
**Author:** Pelle Nilsson
Lake Kussjö, Västerbotten, 2018

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## THE CATACOMB ROGUE

Your name is **Kargunt**.

-   **HP:** 15
-   **Silver:** 15 + d6
-   You may carry unlimited items.

### Starting Equipment

Roll **1d4** on each table:

#### A. Weapon (1d4)

  d4   Weapon
  ---- ------------------------
  1    Warhammer (d6)
  2    Dagger (d4, +1 attack)
  3    Sword (d6, +1 attack)
  4    Flail (d6+1)

#### B. Item (1d4)

  d4   Item
  ---- ----------------------------
  1    Armor (-d4 damage)
  2    Potion (heal d6 HP)
  3    Scroll: Summon weak daemon
  4    Cloak of invisibility (avoid d4 fights while acquiring all monster points)

## ENTRANCE ROOM

Draw a room of any size or shape with **d4 doors** leading outward.

Roll **1d4**:

  d4   Result
  ---- -----------------------------------------------
  1    Find a random (d6) **Item**
  2    A **Weak Monster** stands guard. Attack!
  3    A dying mystic gives a random (d4) **Scroll**
  4    The entrance is eerily quiet

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## MONSTERS

There are **Weak** and **Tough** monsters.

### Combat Rules

-   Roll **d6** to hit.
-   Tie or surpass the monster's **Points** to hit.
-   On success: deal weapon damage (d4-1 if unarmed).
-   On failure: you are hit.
-   Armor absorbs **d4 damage**.
-   Slain monsters grant their **Points**.
-   At **15 Points + 12 explored rooms**, you level up.
-   0 HP = death.
-   You may flee: take **d4 damage**, room remains unexplored.

## LEVELING UP

You level up if:

-   You explore 12 rooms (including Entrance) **and** have 15+ Points
    **OR**
-   You collect 40 silver and give it to the poor of ruined Wästland

When leveling, roll **1d6** and scratch result off list:

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  d6                   Benefit
  -------------------- --------------------------------------------------
  1                    Knighted (Sir/Lady Kargunt)

  2                    +1 attack permanently

  3                    Max HP becomes 20

  4                    Gain 5 potions

  5                    Gain Mighty Zweihänder

  6                    Choose 1 Weak + 1 Tough monster; their damage is
                       halved permanently
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

When all results are scratched off, you retire until the **7th Misery**.

**Congratulations.**
